Interesting Facts about Chameleon

Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of the iguana suborder
  2. Chameleons skin color is an important part of communication among these chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tall that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
